Propergration

The Propagation Cube represents a modern alternative to traditional rock wool or peat moss blocks for seed germination and seeding in hydroponic and horticultural setups. These cubes, featuring pre-cut plug holes, are extensively utilized during the initial growth stages of plants.

By fostering steady root growth and ensuring firm bonding between the roots and tubes, these grow cubes significantly reduce the rejection rate of nursery plants or seeds when transitioning to larger grow bags or pots. Suitable for a wide range of crops including tomatoes, cucumbers, and bell peppers, these grow cubes are easily manageable and can be directly planted onto growing media or grow slabs.
