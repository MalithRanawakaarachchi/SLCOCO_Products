Our Factory

Situated in 262/2 Udawewagama, Hengamuwa, Kobeigane, Sri Lanka, SLCOCO Products (Pvt) Ltd benefits from its strategic location within the coconut triangle of the country. This prime setting ensures access to abundant coconut cultivation areas and optimal raw material supply. Leveraging modern technology, we specialize in transforming coconut fruit into an extensive array of products, including coir fiber pith, grow bags, planter bags, gerbera discs, coco peat bricks, and propagation cubes.

Manufacturing Process

De-husking

The process involves separating coconut husks from the harvested coconuts to extract coir coco peat, also known as coco pith, which resembles sawdust in appearance.

Washing

The E.C. level of coir coco peat is reduced to the required level by thoroughly washing it with fresh water.

Drying

The washed coconut peat is dried in the open sun, typically on a concrete drying yard.

Compacting

Compressing dried and clean coco peat using machinery to produce blocks of the required size.

Testing

The compacted coir coco peat samples undergo rigorous testing through a stringent quality control process to ensure that the E.C levels meet the required specifications.

Transportation

The quality control approved load of coir coco peat blocks is then packed and transported to its destination.

At our facility, we specialize in compressing dried and clean coco peat using advanced machinery. This process ensures the production of high-quality blocks that meet the precise size requirements of our clients. Our commitment to excellence guarantees that each block is crafted to the highest standards, ensuring superior performance for various applications.
