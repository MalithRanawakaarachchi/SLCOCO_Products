Tech Info

SLCOCO Products stands out as a premier manufacturer of Coco-pith Products, including Coco peat, coir fiber pith, coir dust, and coir. Recognized as an exporter by the Export Development Board, Sri Lanka, we are dedicated to delivering top-notch, eco-friendly coir-based substrates to professional growers worldwide. Situated within the renowned "Coconut Triangle" in the North Western province of Sri Lanka, and strategically located 88 km from the Colombo International Airport and Harbor, our factory ensures efficient distribution of our products across the globe.


We remain at the forefront of the ever-evolving modern technology in the global horticulture market, continually striving to pioneer crop-specific media tailored to meet the unique needs of growers. This commitment strengthens our close relationships with all our customers, ensuring their satisfaction and success.


To uphold our commitment to delivering high-quality products, our core strategy revolves around maintaining technical and technological superiority, innovating to meet our customers' diverse requirements, and ensuring competitive pricing. We meticulously verify the quality and standards of our products through regular sample testing procedures conducted in our in-house laboratory. Additionally, we adhere to stringent phytosanitary measures and customer-agreed specifications, further reinforcing our dedication to excellence. Our paramount goal is to produce products of superior quality and standards that captivate buyers, fostering enduring relationships built on trust and satisfaction.
